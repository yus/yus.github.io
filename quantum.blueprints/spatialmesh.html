Architecture Overview

The Spatial Mesh Computing Architecture represents a paradigm shift from traditional von Neumann architectures by distributing computation across a 3D mesh of interconnected nodes.

Each node processes information locally and communicates with its immediate neighbors, enabling massively parallel computation and emergent computational behaviors.
