How it works: Conway's Game of Life creates a flow field that influences boid movement. Boids are represented as triangles that follow the flow field. Boids can also interact with the cellular automaton by changing cell states when they pass over them.

Current Palette: Sunset

Rules: Cells with 2-3 live neighbors survive, cells with exactly 3 neighbors become alive, others die. "Live" means state > 0.
