About Escher's Reptiles

M.C. Escher's "Reptiles" (1943) features interlocking lizard shapes that create a perfect tessellation. The lizards fit together without gaps, exemplifying Escher's mastery of mathematical art.

This black and white version highlights the clean lines and geometric precision of Escher's original design.
