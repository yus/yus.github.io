Conway's Game of Life with Fading Cells

A visualization of the classic cellular automaton with smooth fading transitions between states. Each cell's color represents its current state and age.

Rules of the Game:

  • Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies (underpopulation)
  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors lives on
  • Any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies (overpopulation)
  • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes a live cell (reproduction)

Each cell fades in and out based on its state transitions, creating a smooth visual effect.
